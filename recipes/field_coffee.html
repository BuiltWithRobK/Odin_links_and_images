Field Coffee by BuiltWithRobK

Description

The field coffee is inspired by all my time in the woods. Nothing gets a day going out in the woods quite like a solid cup of coffee. Whether it is the coffee itself or the process of making it, it is a complete moral booster or just the perfect slow start to the day.


Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ground coffee of choice
  • Jetboil or equivalent
  • Gas for Jetboil
  • Field French Press
  • Your favorite coffee mug

Put it all together

  1. Bring water to a light boil using Jetboil
  2. Pour water into french press
  3. Add coffee per your taste (two to three "scoups")
  4. Lightly agitate to get all grounds in the water
  5. Let it sit for 3-4 min and then stir one last time
  6. Press coffee, pour, and enjoy
