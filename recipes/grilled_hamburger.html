Grilled Hamburger by BuiltWithRobK

Description

Built to be the best simple hamburger with the most flavor while still being easy to prep and easy to eat. Take care not to over cook, as this can throw the flavor off. Inspired by an easy way to gather together over a simple meal. It's intended to be shared and enjoyed with friends... It adds to the flavor.


Ingredients

  • Buns
  • 1lb lean meat
  • Lowry's seasoning salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Bread crumbs
  • Egg over easy
  • Bacon
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Cheese of choice (Munster recomended)
